## Common Quote Structure

- Header cells: quote date, job no, quote no, production company, production, contact, location, shoot dates, Bickers contact.
- Line-item grid: `DESCRIPTION`, `QTY`, `UNIT PRICE`, `TOTAL`.
- Main sections: equipment daily rates, labour daily rates, travel charges, accommodation/meals, discounts, total price.
- Most charge rows calculate as `QTY * UNIT PRICE`; optional rows use `TBC`, `Production`, or blank quantity.
- Footer total generally sums the charge rows and excludes VAT.

- Still needed from the business side: vehicle-to-template mapping, quote-number generation rules, and which optional/TBC lines should be included by default.
